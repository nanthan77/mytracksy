❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept PayHere (for Sri Lankan users), international credit/debit cards via Stripe, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.
Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.
Is there a free trial available?
Our Starter plan is free forever with generous limits. For paid plans, we offer a 7-day free trial with full access to all features.
Do you offer discounts for students or startups?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified students, 75% off for startups (first 6 months), and 60% off for registered NGOs. Contact us for verification.
Is my financial data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, secure servers, and never store your banking passwords. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
