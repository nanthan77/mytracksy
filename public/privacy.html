Privacy Policy

MyTracksy is built around privacy-first financial workflows and Firebase-backed application infrastructure.

What data may be stored

  • Account identity such as email, display name, profession, and onboarding preferences.
  • Operational and financial records you create, including transactions, invoices, schedules, notes, and profession-specific workflow data.
  • Usage and diagnostic information needed to keep security, subscriptions, and app performance working correctly.

How data is used

  • To authenticate users and protect access to their workspace.
  • To power reporting, AI-assisted workflows, reminders, and profession-specific dashboards.
  • To support support, billing, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

Your responsibility

If you upload regulated personal or client data, you are responsible for using MyTracksy in a way that matches your legal and professional obligations.
