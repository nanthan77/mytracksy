MyTracksy is architected for the regulatory reality of Sri Lankan medical practice — where patient data is special-category information and your compliance posture has to be more than a badge.

Built for PDPA-Ready Operations — Personal Data Protection Act, No. 9 of 2022
System Integrity
PDPA-Aligned Data Handling
Designed for handling special-category medical data with role-based access controls, encryption in transit and at rest, contractual restrictions on public-model training, and auditable processing controls throughout the platform.
Privacy Controls
Automated Redaction & Pseudonymization
Patient-identifier redaction and pseudonymization workflows are applied based on your deployment policy and workflow configuration — giving you control over how identifiable data is handled across the platform.
Voice Processing
Ephemeral Voice Handling
Voice inputs are processed using ephemeral controls with vendor restrictions designed to minimize retention and prevent use for public model training. No voice data is stored beyond the active processing session under normal operating conditions.
Cloud Infrastructure
Enterprise Cloud with Cross-Border Safeguards
Hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure in Singapore and Mumbai, with cross-border processing safeguards, end-to-end encryption, audit logging, and contractual data-protection controls aligned with PDPA cross-border transfer requirements.
Payment Processing
Sri Lanka-Licensed Payment Channels
Integrates with Sri Lanka-licensed and authorized payment providers and acquiring channels, subject to merchant onboarding and availability. All payment flows are processed through regulated financial infrastructure.
Tax Logic
IRD-Aligned Tax Estimation
Tax-estimation workflows are built against applicable Sri Lankan tax rules and designed for periodic review by qualified Sri Lankan tax professionals, including members of CA Sri Lanka and IRD-recognized tax-agent categories.
MyTracksy is designed to support compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act, No. 9 of 2022 . Compliance status depends on your organization's deployment configuration, operational policies, and ongoing governance practices. Feature availability, payment channel access, and tax-rule coverage may vary based on region, provider onboarding, and regulatory updates.
