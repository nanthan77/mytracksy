# How Do I Request a Product Catalog from Globe Multi Traders?

You can request a free PDF product catalog by contacting us via WhatsApp or phone. Once you reach out, our team will send you the latest catalog directly as a PDF download.

## How to Request Your Catalog

### Option 1: WhatsApp

1. Save our WhatsApp number: **[Insert WhatsApp Number]**
2. Send a message saying: *"I'd like to receive the product catalog"*
3. Include your **name** and **business name** (if applicable)
4. Our team will reply with the latest PDF catalog, usually within **1 business day**

### Option 2: Phone Call

1. Call us at: **[Insert Phone Number]**
2. Ask for the **sales team** and request the product catalog
3. Provide your **email address** so we can send the PDF to you
4. You'll receive the catalog via email shortly after the call

## What's Included in the Catalog?

The Globe Multi Traders product catalog includes:

- Full product listings with descriptions and images
- Available quantities and packaging details
- Pricing information (bulk and wholesale rates where applicable)
- Product categories for easy browsing
