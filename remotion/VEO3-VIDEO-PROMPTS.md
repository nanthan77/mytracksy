## 📝 Veo 3 Best Practices

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Resolution** | Always specify "4K" or "8K" quality in prompts |
| **Frame Rate** | Use "24fps" for cinematic, "30fps" for smoother motion |
| **Duration** | 5-6 seconds is the sweet spot for social media clips |
| **Camera** | Specify camera movement (push in, orbit, static, dolly) |
| **Lighting** | Mention specific lighting (cinematic, ambient, rim light) |
| **Physics** | Describe particle behavior, reflections, and materials |
| **Consistency** | Keep the dark indigo + teal brand palette in every prompt |
| **Image-to-Video** | Upload your generated image as the "starting frame" in Veo 3 |
